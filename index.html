About Us



India faces a critical challenge: homelessness, a complex issue fueled by soaring housing costs, limited affordable options, and a fragile social safety net. Homeless Connect emerges as a beacon of hope, offering a holistic approach that goes beyond temporary shelter.


Here's what sets us apart:

  • Nationwide Network: We leverage a network of partners across India, ensuring culturally sensitive support and access to local resources.
  • One-Stop Resource Center: Find curated job opportunities tailored to the Indian market, access educational programs for upskilling, and participate in life skills workshops all in one place.
  • Empowerment Through Resources: We equip individuals with the tools they need to build a secure future.


Homeless Connect is more than just a program; we are weavers of hope, working tirelessly to dismantle the barriers that perpetuate homelessness and pave a path towards a future where everyone has the chance to thrive.







Our Services:

Local Expertise, National Reach:


Our program leverages a nationwide network of partner organizations, shelters, and government agencies. This ensures access to local resources and expertise, while also benefiting from the strength and reach of a pan-India network.

Culturally Sensitive Support:


Our dedicated caseworkers come from diverse backgrounds and understand the specific social and cultural nuances that can impact individuals experiencing homelessness in India.

One-Stop Resource Center:


Eliminate the hassle of navigating multiple agencies. Homeless Connect provides a centralized platform where you can access a variety of resources under one roof. Find housing options, employment opportunities, educational programs, legal aid services, and crucial support services – all in one place.

Empowerment Through Resources:


We offer a job portal with curated listings tailored to the Indian job market. Additionally, we provide resume building assistance, interview coaching, and job training opportunities to equip you with the skills needed to launch a successful career.

Invest in Your Future:


Upskill and enhance your employability through partnerships with local educational institutions. We offer adult education programs, vocational training courses relevant to the Indian market, and certificate programs that can open doors to new career opportunities.

Holistic Support Services:


Homeless Connect recognizes that your needs extend beyond just housing. We offer connections to mental health and addiction services, financial literacy training to manage your finances effectively, and life skills workshops that foster independence and self-sufficiency.
